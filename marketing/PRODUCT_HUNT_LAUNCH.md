**Hi Product Hunt community! 👋**

I’m thrilled to introduce **LinkGuardian**. 

As a creator or a business owner, your social media bio link is often the *only* gateway to your revenue. Most creators only find out their link is down hours—or even days—after it breaks, usually from an angry follower.

That’s why we built **LinkGuardian**. 

We wanted a "set-and-forget" solution that watches our backs 24/7.

*   **🌍 Global Monitoring:** Our automated bots check your links every hour from multiple global locations.
*   **🚨 Instant Email Alerts:** The moment a link fails (404 error, server down), you get pinged instantly so you can fix it before your audience even notices.
*   **📊 Performance Analytics:** Real-time uptime graphs and global availability metrics in a beautiful, simple dashboard.
*   **💳 Seamless Upgrades:** Start for free, and upgrade to PRO using PayPal from anywhere in the world when you need unlimited monitoring.
